Plans & free time

Everything you need to fill a weekend — le ciné, la rando, l’expo, and the little words like libre and dispo that make plans happen. Pairs with lesson 6, Making plans.

How to use the deck

Tap a card to flip it; tap the speaker to hear it. Notice how many of these are chopped-short words — ciné, resto, expo, rando — the short form is the natural one.

Put them to work

T’es dispo samedi ? Y’a une expo super au musée.

“Free Saturday? There’s a great exhibition at the museum.” Deck words + lesson 6 = a plan.

Ce week-end : rando le samedi, ciné le dimanche.

“This weekend: a hike Saturday, cinema Sunday.” Note le samedi — the article makes it “on Saturday”.

The great escape phrase

« J’ai déjà un truc de prévu » — “I’ve already got something on” — declines any plan without explanation or guilt. No follow-up questions will come; it’s a social convention, and everyone respects the mysterious truc.
