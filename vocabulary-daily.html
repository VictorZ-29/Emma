Daily life & routine

The words your ordinary day is made of — le boulot, les courses, la grasse matinée. This is the deck that makes “tell me about your day” answerable.

How to use the deck

Tap a card to flip it; tap the speaker to hear it. Nouns come with their article — learn them as one word, and the gender comes free.

Put them to work

Journée classique : boulot, courses, et une série le soir.

“Standard day: work, shopping, and a series in the evening.” An honest, fluent-sounding answer to « t’as fait quoi aujourd’hui ? »

Dimanche ? Grasse mat’ et rien d’autre.

“Sunday? Lie-in and nothing else.” The French respect this answer deeply.

The « faire » routine

Half of daily life runs on faire: faire les courses, faire le ménage, faire la lessive, faire la grasse matinée. Bundle them mentally — “chores = faire + the thing”.
